Cost Of Studying In The France:

Accommodation: The cost of accommodation depends on whether you live in university housing, private rented accommodation, or with a host family. On average, you might spend anywhere from 300 to 800 euros per month.

Food: Monthly grocery expenses could range from 150 to 250 euros, while eating out at restaurants may cost around 10 to 20 euros per meal.

Transportation: Public transportation costs vary by city but can range from 30 to 70 euros per month.

Health Insurance: Health insurance costs are mandatory and may range from 200 to 600 euros per year.

Miscellaneous: Additional expenses for books, supplies, and personal items may be around 50 to 100 euros per month.

Visa Fees: Non-EU/EEA students may need to pay visa fees, typically around 99 euros.
